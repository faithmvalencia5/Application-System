About OSCA Bauan

The Office of Senior Citizens Affairs (OSCA) Bauan is committed to promoting the welfare, rights, and empowerment of senior citizens. Established under the Expanded Senior Citizens Act, we provide accessible services, social pension distribution, health programs, and community engagement initiatives.

We honor the wisdom and contributions of our elderly population, ensuring they live with dignity, security, and opportunities for active participation.
